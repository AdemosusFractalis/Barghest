A Plague Tale Story

I will consider the game's story is over for this review although i heard it could be a third opus one day.

OST : 9/10

I think the ost in the game is definitely one of the best I ever heard although i think in the second opus the ost did not strike me as well as I expected even though was very cool and epic.

Gameplay : 7.5/10

I think the gameplay is very enjoyable if you use a controller or keyboard but fight sessions are very repetitive in Requiem and you CANNOT choose to play it full furtive because they are too many enemies in some maps. The enigmas(puzzles) are too easy for me.The bosses are rare and not very enjoyable fights sometimes it's quite deceptive (the Count, Nicholas..)

Story : 9.5/10

Definitely the best part of this game, the story is very engaging and even more scarier in the second opus. You really love the characters and they become part of the gameplay and enlighten the immersion of the game. All protagonists and antagonists are well-written. Story very tragic and emotional. Definitely loved it.

Graphics/Artistic Direction : 9/10

Lets be clear : the first game gets 8.5 then the second one 9.5 or even 10. The designers did a great job i think. Nights phases as well are day phases are wonderful. The lightings and ambient occlusion are perfectly driven. Character style is very realistic but not too much, all characters are badass, animations are very correct.

Global : 8.75/10

It's the kind of immersive movie you must have played at least once in your life. I never felt i lost my time playing this masterpiece. You can play this in one easy mode just to enjoy the movie or hard mode if you are a frenetic hardcore gamer (be clear : it's an easy game, people that never play video games could beat this one). I think anyone can find its account in this game, except if you find only super-intensive souls-like gameplay and don't mind about story.
